About

Tech for Everyone is a community-driven event that brings together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from all backgrounds. Our mission is to make technology accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level.

Organized by GDG Algiers, we aim to foster learning, networking, and innovation in the tech community.
